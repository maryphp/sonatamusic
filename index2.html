最新动态

Private Music Course As students build an appreciation and enjoyment of music, private lessons are an excellent way to train for technical proficiency and performance. The ideal music education experience combines a mixture of both private and ensemble formats to exploit the strengths of each format. Although private lessons allow students some exposure to the principles and techniques that cultivate a love for playing and composing music, the focus and strength of one-on-one interaction is the cultivation of technique and the lesson intensity geared towards the capacity of the individual.
